The online supply and demand platform for artists and clients.

Welcome to Custom Artist

Custom Artist creates opportunities for artists to get in touch with clients. These clients could be companies or private clients who are looking for an artist to create a particular form of artwork.

The client uploads an assignment. Artists respond to assignments they’re interested in with an artwork proposal. Through a logical and clear step-by-step process the artist and client can come to an agreement.

Custom Artist supports artists who are struggling to find assignments to earn money. At the same time it saves companies and private clients time to find the art they are – in whatever form – looking for.

How does it work?

Artist or Client?

You can your way in the Navigation Bar at the top of the page. If you're an artist looking for assignments, click on 'Artists'. If you're a client who wants to post an assignment, click on 'Clients'. Be sure to register, and you're good to go.

If you're an Artist

1. Find an Assignment

You can find available assignments on the 'Assignments' page. Find something that you like, and click on 'View Details'.

2. Read Description

You can view an extensive description of the assignment. Do you feel like you are up to the job? Click on 'Submit a Proposal'.

3. Submit your Proposal

Enter the details of your proposal and when you're done, submit it! If the client likes your proposal, they will contact you. Well done!

If you're a Client

1. Add an Assignment

Create a new assignment and add a detailed description. Submit it, and it will be posted for artists to see.

2. Wait for Proposals

Wait for an artist to make a proposal for your assignment. You can view the status of your running assignments by clicking on 'My Assignments' and viewing the details.

3. Contact the Artist

If you like a proposal, contact the artist to make an appointment and discuss the details of your assignment. Done!
